Creamy Mango Graham Bar Recipe

Ingredients

  • 410 ml Kremdensada (sweetened condensed milk), chilled overnight
  • 125 ml all-purpose cream, chilled
  • 24 pieces graham crackers
  • 3 small sweet mangoes, diced into cubes

Preparation Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the chilled Kremdensada and all-purpose cream.
  2. Beat the mixture on high speed until it doubles in volume—this creates a light and fluffy cream base.
  3. Purchase or prepare a suitable container; for example, a 3-liter Graham Bar container works well for 12 bars.
  4. Arrange a layer of graham crackers evenly at the bottom of the container.
  5. Pour half of the whipped cream mixture over the crackers and smooth it out with a spatula.
  6. Distribute the diced mango cubes evenly over the cream layer.
  7. Pour in the remaining whipped cream and flatten it to create an even layer.
  8. Add another layer of graham crackers on top.
  9. Cover the container with a lid and place it in the freezer overnight to set firmly.
  10. Once frozen, unmold the dessert and slice it into individual bars.
  11. Wrap each bar in foil—fold and seal both ends to keep them fresh.
